Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

ScoreKeeper ("the App") is a score-tracking and game-utility application developed for iPhone and iPad. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information the App handles and how.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

All data you create inside the App — player names, scores, game history, and settings — is stored exclusively in local storage on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through iCloud or iTunes (governed by Apple's own privacy policies).

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Player names and avatars you create
  • Game scores and session history
  • Game settings and preferences
  • App usage stats (e.g. how often you play a game) — used only for sorting your frequently played games

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. There are no ads, no in-app purchase paywalls, and no telemetry of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

iCloud & Device Backups

If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your local app data may be included in those backups. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to that data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
